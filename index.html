📈 The Progression System
✅ Hit the top rep on all 4 sets with clean form? Add 5 lbs next session. That's how you get bigger week by week.
✅ Log every lift in your Notes app. "RDL — 75 lbs × 10." Do this every night. In 8 weeks you'll have proof of your growth.
✅ Tournament week, back-to-back heavy days, body feels broken? Skip weights. Sleep extra. Eat more. Recovery IS the workout.

Faith First — Every Morning

Prayer, reading, journaling — whatever your practice is. The days you're tempted to skip it are exactly the days you need it most. A busy schedule isn't a reason to cut it — it's a reason to anchor yourself before the chaos starts.
💼
Your Reselling Business — Treat It Like One
You already have something most people your age don't — an income stream you built yourself. Now it's time to be intentional with it. Block 30–45 minutes every weekday — same time, same focus. Sourcing, listing, shipping, customer service, tracking what sells. Saturday is your deep work day — 1–2 hours building and planning. Write down your revenue goal for the 8 weeks. What you measure, you improve. Treat your business like a job and it'll pay you like one.
📝
ACT/SAT — This Is Athletic Training Too
You go to group basketball training twice a week because you know reps make you better. Test prep is the same thing. Show up to Tuesday and Thursday prep the same way you show up to the gym — ready to work, not just to be there. Eat before you go. Your brain needs fuel. The score you build over these 8 weeks opens doors your game alone can't. Treat it like the investment it is.
🏀
Basketball — You're Building a Complete Player
At 5'11 and 140 lbs right now, adding 10 lbs of real muscle will change your game. You'll be harder to move in the post. Your first step will be more explosive. You'll finish through contact instead of getting knocked off your line. The Bulgarian split squats and box jumps on Fridays are directly for your game — not just aesthetics. Every lift you do is basketball training in disguise.
😴
Sleep — Your Most Powerful Tool
You're growing, training hard, studying, and running a business. 9 hours of sleep is not optional — it's the engine that makes all of it work. In bed by 10:30pm every night. No screens 30 minutes before bed. Your muscles grow during sleep. Your brain consolidates what you studied during sleep. Your body regulates hormones during sleep. Cutting sleep to fit more in is like draining the gas tank to make the car lighter. It doesn't work.
